COVID-19 affects everyone. Infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and may recover without hospitalization.

Symptoms Preventions Treatments

Most common symptoms:  fever, dry cough, tiredness
Less common symptoms:  aches and pains, sore throat, diarrhoea, ,conjunctivitis ,headache ,loss of taste or smell ,a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes

Clean your hands often with soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub. Maintain a safe distance from everyone. Donâ€™t touch your eyes, nose or mouth. Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow when you cough or sneeze. If having fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention  

If you feel sick take rest, drink plenty of fluid, and eat nutritious food. Stay in a separate room from other family members, and use a dedicated bathroom if possible. Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces. It is normal to feel sad, stressed, or confused during a crisis talk to a health worker or counsellor.
